Detailed Description

This market conditions simulator emulates exercising of short option positions in the portfolio. Simulator implements basic no-arb argument: when time value of the option contract is close to zero it assigns short legs getting profit close to expiration dates in deep ITM positions. User algorithm then receives assignment event from LEAN. Simulator randomly scans for arbitrage opportunities every two hours or so.
